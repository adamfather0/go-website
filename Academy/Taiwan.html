About Haifeng

In 1997, Mr. Lin Wenbo, driven by his passion for Go, decided to donate thirty million NT dollars to establish a foundation dedicated to promoting Go. After consulting with national Go player Lin Haifeng, who gave his full support and agreed to serve as the first chairman, the "Haifeng Cultural and Educational Foundation" was established, initiating its mission to promote Go. Due to the significant financial and manpower requirements for organizing professional and amateur competitions, the Haifeng Foundation has been substantially supported by Chairman Lin Wenbo over the years, leading to rapid growth. However, the scope of professional competitions was still insufficient, necessitating organizational adjustments. Chairman Lin decided to merge the "Haifeng Cultural and Educational Foundation" into the "Peison Cultural and Educational Foundation" to leverage synergies.

The Peison Cultural and Educational Foundation, another corporate entity funded by Chairman Lin Wenbo and his family, has been actively involved in special education. With more abundant resources than the Haifeng Cultural and Educational Foundation, the merger allowed the Haifeng Foundation to hand over the promotion of Go to the Peison Foundation. The staff of the original Haifeng Foundation were also reorganized to meet the needs of Go promotion, forming the "Haifeng Go Institute" to continue working on the front lines of Go promotion.
